How to Get the Most out of Playing eFootball World

eFootball World is at the very core of the eFootball experience. You can play with Authentic Teams to recreate your favourite real-life rivalries.

On the other hand, you can create your Creative Team by signing and developing players of your choice.

MAIN FEATURES

B. Creative Team

Build your dream team and face off against users from around the globe. Sign players and managers that fit your chosen formation and tactics, and develop them to their full potential. In eFootbal 2022 you'll be able to target the signings you covet the most, and you can also develop the player as you see fit. This all means that when the final whistle is blown, it rests on your shoulders whether you step off the pitch winners or losers! So get out there and make your dream team a reality!

C. Match Passes

With every match you play, you'll have the chance to earn rewards like the "Nominating Contract", which allows you to sign a player of your choosing, among other items. You can purchase an "Advantage Match Pass" using eFootball Coins to get even more items.

D. Objective

You can receive amazing rewards for completing objectives.

Each objective will have its own reward, so make sure you do your best to complete as many as you can.

If you want even better rewards, try completing Premium Objectives, which are unlocked using eFootball Coins.

E. Contracts

Chance Deal : An item that allows you to sign a random player from a list of players tailored for this deal.

Nominating Contract     : An item that allows you to sign a player of your choosing from a list of players tailored for this contract.

F. Virtual Currency

- eFootball Coins: An in-game currency you can use to sign players and purchase Advantage Match Passes, among other items.

- GP : An in-game currency you can use to sign players and managers.

- eFootball Points : In-game points you can redeem for player signings and items.

G. Player Types

There are 4 types of players in the game: Standard, Trending, Featured and Legendary.

1. Standard : Players are based on their performance during the current season. 

- Player development : Yes. 

- How to sign : eFootball Coins (Random), GP (Nominated), and Chance Deal.


2. Trending : Players are based on a specific match or week in which they put an impressive performance during this season.

- Player development : No.

- How to sign : Nominating Contract.

3. Featured : Hand-picked players that are depicted based on their performance during the current season.

- Player development : Yes,

- How to sign : eFootball Coins (Random), Chance Deal, and eFootball Points (Nominated).

4. Legendary : Players are based on a specific season when they had a stellar performance (This also includes retired players who had a brilliant career).

- Player development : Yes.

- eFootball Coins (Random), Chance Deal, and eFootball Points (Nominated).

H. How to Sign Players

To sign a player, you'll need virtual currency or an item like a "Chance Deal".

I. How to Develop Players

Trending players are versions of players who have put in excellent performances on a particular week and have had their stats boosted to reflect that performance. This type of player is perfect for immediate inclusion on your team sheet, but can't be developed any further.

Standard, Featured and Legendary players can all be trained to boost their stats and fine-tune their abilities to fit your game plan. They may not start with impressive stats like their Trending counterparts, but given enough time to develop they may eventually become a better fit for your team!

Therefore, develop your players via Training; Fine-tune them to better suit your game plan and guide your team to glory. Moreover, additional Training Programs will be introduced in the future to provide further development options for your players.

J. Match Types

There are 5 types of matches as described below.

1. Tour Event : Play against AI opponents in a tour format, earning rewards by accumulating Event Points.

2. Challenge Event :Play against human opponents online, completing assigned mission objectives to earn rewards.

3. Online Quick Match : Play a casual online match against a human opponent.

4. Online Match Lobby : Create an online match room and invite an opponent for a 1vs1 match.

5. eFootball Creative League : Use Creative Teams to play against the creme de la creme of eFootball World.

Play PvP matches against evenly-matched opponents and gather points to rise the Divisions.

You will gain rewards based on your performance during a Round (10 matches) and your final standing at the end of a Phase.
